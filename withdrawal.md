## Step-by-Step

### STEP 1: USDC → XLM on ChangeNOW

1. Go to [changenow.io](https://changenow.io) (no signup, no KYC)
2. Send: **USDC** on **Polygon** network
3. Receive: **XLM** (Stellar)
4. Enter your XLM wallet address (from KuCoin/OKX or a wallet like Guarda)
5. Copy the deposit address they give you
6. Send USDC from your Polymarket wallet to that address
7. Wait 2-5 minutes — XLM arrives in your wallet

**Why XLM?** Fastest (3-5 sec finality), cheapest ($0.00001 fee), available on every exchange.

### STEP 2: XLM → KuCoin P2P

1. Sign up on [KuCoin](https://www.kucoin.com) — email only, no KYC needed for spot/P2P
2. Go to **Assets → Deposit → XLM**
3. Copy your XLM deposit address + memo tag
4. Send XLM from ChangeNOW to this address
5. KuCoin receives it in 30 seconds

### STEP 3: P2P Sell to Indian Buyer

1. Go to **KuCoin → P2P Trading**
2. Select **Sell USDT → INR**
3. Filter merchants by:
   - **Orders completed:** 500+ (experienced)
   - **30-day completion rate:** 98%+ (reliable)
   - **Payment method:** UPI (instant)
4. Choose a buyer offering good rate (~₹83-86 per USDT)
5. Enter amount (₹10K-50K max per trade)
6. Click Sell → your USDT gets locked
7. Buyer sends ₹ to your UPI ID
8. Check your UPI app — once received, click **Confirm Release**
9. USDT released to buyer, money in your bank

**Time:** 2-5 minutes per trade

### STEP 4: Move Money Around

- Keep in the UPI account you received it in
- Transfer to main bank account in chunks of ₹5K-10K
- Or just spend directly from the UPI account
